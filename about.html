Who We Are

A Record That Speaks for Itself

Founded in September of 2003, VLink Express Courier Ltd just celebrated its sixteenth anniversary as the highest quality provider of shipping services this side of the Baltic Sea. After sixteen years of helping our customers sail or fly their shipment the world over, and arranging the handling, servicing, and customs clearing, we have grown from a small shipping agency to a full provider of complete shipping services. We excel at the logistics business, because we are more than a courier company. We are a solutions company powered as much by brainpower as by horsepower. Together, over 7,000 of our problem-solvers ensure your shipment moves as efficiently and safely as possible.

VLink’s service standards are second to none in the industry, and the company is continually searching for new ways to improve service to our customers. All members of the VLink team – drivers, contractors, managers, couriers, clerks and mechanics – take pride in providing reasonable rates and dependable deliveries. The company is focused on the safety of its employees, equipment, shipments and the general public. VLink Express Courier Ltd has a specific department to cater to each customer’s shipping needs. Whether you require an international freight service, customs clearing, domestic shipping or secure storage of items, a team of specialists at VLink will see that your specific needs are met in the most cost-effective way possible.

By staying true to our core ideals, VLink Express Courier Ltd has achieved exponential growth. Eager to harness the power of technology for the good of our customers and our employees, VLink was one of the first courier company to fully integrate satellite communications aboard the fleet. In fact, we surpassed $300 million in annual revenue faster than any teenage courier company in history. In the process, our fleet has grown from 32 vans to 16 ocean liners, 56 planes, 6,700 motorcycles and 13,200 vans. And the innovative spirit that first launched VLink Express Courier Ltd is now fueled by over 7,000 solutions-minded problem- solvers. So no matter what new challenges the future may bring, you can count on US.
